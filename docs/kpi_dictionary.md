# KPI Dictionary

## Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators used in the Asset-Finance Operations Intelligence System.

Each KPI includes its definition, calculation logic, reporting frequency and intended business use.

## Collections and Repayment KPIs

| KPI                                   | Definition                                                                              | Calculation                                                                                     | Frequency | Business Use                                         |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Expected Collections                  | Total repayment amount contractually due during the reporting period                    | Sum of weekly repayment obligations due                                                         | Weekly    | Measures the amount the business expected to collect |
| Actual Collections                    | Total repayment amount successfully recovered during the reporting period               | Sum of repayments applied to rider obligations                                                  | Weekly    | Measures actual repayment performance                |
| Collection Rate                       | Percentage of expected collections successfully recovered                               | Actual Collections / Expected Collections × 100                                                 | Weekly    | Evaluates collection efficiency                      |
| Collection Variance                   | Difference between expected and actual collections                                      | Actual Collections - Expected Collections                                                       | Weekly    | Shows over- or under-collection                      |
| Outstanding Balance                   | Total unpaid obligations remaining at the end of the reporting period                   | Previous Balance + Current Obligations - Amount Recovered                                       | Weekly    | Measures unpaid rider debt                           |
| Average Outstanding Balance per Rider | Average debt held by riders with outstanding balances                                   | Total Outstanding Balance / Number of Riders with Outstanding Balances                          | Weekly    | Shows average debt exposure                          |
| Partial Repayment Count               | Number of riders who paid less than the amount due                                      | Count of riders where Actual Repayment is greater than 0 but less than Expected Repayment       | Weekly    | Identifies riders who made incomplete payments       |
| Missed Repayment Count                | Number of riders who made no repayment during a period when payment was due             | Count of riders where Expected Repayment is greater than 0 and Actual Repayment equals 0        | Weekly    | Supports collections monitoring                      |
| Consecutive Missed Repayments         | Number of continuous reporting periods in which a rider missed repayment                | Count of consecutive weeks with no repayment while an obligation was due                        | Weekly    | Supports risk classification                         |
| Contract Completion Rate              | Percentage of total contract obligation already paid                                    | Cumulative Repayment / Total Contract Value × 100                                               | Weekly    | Measures progress toward contract completion         |
| Contracts Behind Schedule             | Number of contracts where cumulative repayment is below the expected repayment schedule | Count of contracts where Actual Cumulative Repayment is less than Expected Cumulative Repayment | Weekly    | Identifies underperforming contracts                 |

## Earnings and Payout KPIs

| KPI                         | Definition                                                        | Calculation                                                                                   | Frequency | Business Use                                         |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Gross Rider Earnings        | Total earnings generated by a rider before deductions             | Sum of rider earnings for the reporting period                                                | Weekly    | Measures rider earning activity                      |
| Total Deductions            | Total amount deducted from rider earnings                         | Repayment Deduction + Maintenance Deduction + Operational Charges + Previous Balance Recovery | Weekly    | Shows total deductions applied                       |
| Repayment Deduction         | Portion of rider earnings applied to repayment obligations        | Amount deducted toward weekly repayment                                                       | Weekly    | Measures repayment recovery through earnings         |
| Maintenance Deduction       | Amount deducted from rider earnings for maintenance-related costs | Sum of valid maintenance deductions                                                           | Weekly    | Measures maintenance recovery                        |
| Operational Charges         | Total approved non-repayment charges applied to a rider           | Sum of battery, insurance, penalty and other approved charges                                 | Weekly    | Tracks additional operational deductions             |
| Net Rider Payout            | Amount remaining after all valid deductions                       | Gross Earnings - Total Deductions                                                             | Weekly    | Shows the final payable amount to the rider          |
| Zero Payout Count           | Number of riders whose calculated payout is zero                  | Count of riders where Net Rider Payout equals 0                                               | Weekly    | Identifies riders whose earnings were fully absorbed |
| Insufficient Earnings Count | Number of riders whose earnings could not cover total obligations | Count of riders where Gross Earnings is less than Total Obligations                           | Weekly    | Identifies riders likely to carry forward debt       |
| Carried-Forward Balance     | Unpaid obligation transferred to the next reporting period        | Maximum of 0 and Total Obligations - Available Earnings and Payments                          | Weekly    | Tracks debt movement between periods                 |

## Reconciliation KPIs

| KPI                         | Definition                                                                                 | Calculation                                                                    | Frequency | Business Use                                   |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Calculated Payout           | Payout produced by the system using documented business rules                              | Gross Earnings - Valid Deductions                                              | Weekly    | Provides the expected payout                   |
| Recorded Payout             | Payout captured in the source payment or operational record                                | Sum of recorded rider payouts                                                  | Weekly    | Represents the payout recorded by the business |
| Reconciliation Variance     | Difference between calculated and recorded payout                                          | Calculated Payout - Recorded Payout                                            | Weekly    | Identifies payout discrepancies                |
| Reconciled Record Count     | Number of records where calculated and recorded values match within the accepted tolerance | Count of records where absolute variance is within tolerance                   | Weekly    | Measures reconciliation completion             |
| Reconciliation Rate         | Percentage of records successfully reconciled                                              | Reconciled Record Count / Total Records Reviewed × 100                         | Weekly    | Measures the effectiveness of reconciliation   |
| Exception Count             | Number of records that fail reconciliation or validation checks                            | Count of records flagged as exceptions                                         | Weekly    | Measures unresolved reporting issues           |
| Exception Value             | Total financial value associated with reconciliation exceptions                            | Sum of absolute reconciliation variances                                       | Weekly    | Measures financial exposure from exceptions    |
| Duplicate Transaction Count | Number of repeated transaction records                                                     | Count of duplicate transaction references or matching transaction combinations | Weekly    | Detects duplicate postings                     |
| Unmatched Transaction Count | Number of transactions that cannot be linked to a valid rider, contract or source record   | Count of unmatched transactions                                                | Weekly    | Identifies incomplete or invalid records       |
| Manual Review Count         | Number of records requiring human investigation                                            | Count of records classified as manual review                                   | Weekly    | Supports operational workload planning         |

## Fleet and Asset KPIs

| KPI                              | Definition                                                             | Calculation                                          | Frequency | Business Use                                |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Total Assets                     | Total number of assets recorded in the portfolio                       | Count of unique asset IDs                            | Weekly    | Provides the overall portfolio size         |
| Active Assets                    | Assets currently assigned and generating operational activity          | Count of assets with active operating status         | Weekly    | Measures productive fleet size              |
| Idle Assets                      | Deployable assets not currently generating operational activity        | Count of assets classified as idle                   | Weekly    | Identifies underused assets                 |
| Assets Under Maintenance         | Assets unavailable because of open maintenance work                    | Count of assets with active maintenance status       | Weekly    | Measures maintenance-related unavailability |
| Repossessed Assets               | Assets recovered from riders because of contract or operational issues | Count of assets classified as repossessed            | Weekly    | Monitors recovered assets                   |
| Ownership-Completed Assets       | Assets whose rent-to-own contracts have been fully completed           | Count of assets with ownership-completed status      | Weekly    | Tracks contract completion                  |
| Fleet Utilization Rate           | Percentage of deployable assets actively operating                     | Active Assets / Deployable Assets × 100              | Weekly    | Measures asset utilization                  |
| Idle Rate                        | Percentage of deployable assets currently idle                         | Idle Assets / Deployable Assets × 100                | Weekly    | Measures underutilization                   |
| Average Idle Days                | Average number of days assets remain idle                              | Total Idle Days / Number of Idle Assets              | Weekly    | Measures the duration of idle status        |
| Asset Assignment Rate            | Percentage of deployable assets currently assigned to riders           | Assigned Assets / Deployable Assets × 100            | Weekly    | Measures asset deployment                   |
| Multiple Active Assignment Count | Number of assets linked to more than one active rider at the same time | Count of assets with more than one active assignment | Weekly    | Detects assignment control failures         |

## Maintenance and Downtime KPIs

| KPI                                   | Definition                                                             | Calculation                                            | Frequency          | Business Use                    |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------ | ------------------------------- |
| Maintenance Incident Count            | Total number of maintenance incidents recorded                         | Count of unique maintenance records                    | Weekly and Monthly | Measures maintenance volume     |
| Total Maintenance Cost                | Total maintenance expenditure during the reporting period              | Sum of maintenance costs                               | Weekly and Monthly | Measures maintenance spending   |
| Average Maintenance Cost per Incident | Average cost of each maintenance event                                 | Total Maintenance Cost / Maintenance Incident Count    | Monthly            | Measures average repair cost    |
| Average Maintenance Cost per Asset    | Average maintenance cost across assets with maintenance activity       | Total Maintenance Cost / Number of Assets Maintained   | Monthly            | Compares maintenance burden     |
| Maintenance Downtime                  | Number of days an asset is unavailable because of maintenance          | Maintenance End Date - Maintenance Start Date          | Per Incident       | Measures lost operating time    |
| Average Downtime                      | Average maintenance downtime across completed incidents                | Total Downtime Days / Completed Maintenance Incidents  | Monthly            | Measures maintenance turnaround |
| Open Maintenance Cases                | Number of maintenance incidents not yet completed                      | Count of maintenance records with open status          | Weekly             | Identifies pending repairs      |
| Repeat Maintenance Rate               | Percentage of assets returning for maintenance within a defined period | Assets with Repeat Incidents / Assets Maintained × 100 | Monthly            | Detects recurring failures      |
| High-Cost Maintenance Count           | Number of maintenance incidents exceeding the defined cost threshold   | Count of incidents above threshold                     | Monthly            | Highlights costly repairs       |
| Downtime Utilization Loss             | Estimated operating time lost because of maintenance                   | Downtime Days / Available Operating Days × 100         | Monthly            | Measures operational impact     |

| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Risk Score                     | Numerical score based on documented rider-risk factors                    | Weighted total of missed payments, debt trend, earnings decline, inactivity and maintenance incidents | Weekly    | Supports account prioritization       |
| Low-Risk Riders                | Riders meeting low-risk thresholds                                        | Count of riders classified as low risk                                                                | Weekly    | Identifies stable accounts            |
| Medium-Risk Riders             | Riders requiring closer monitoring                                        | Count of riders classified as medium risk                                                             | Weekly    | Supports preventive follow-up         |
| High-Risk Riders               | Riders showing significant repayment or operational risk                  | Count of riders classified as high risk                                                               | Weekly    | Supports prioritized intervention     |
| Critical-Risk Riders           | Riders requiring immediate escalation                                     | Count of riders classified as critical risk                                                           | Weekly    | Supports urgent action                |
| At-Risk Rider Rate             | Percentage of active riders classified as high or critical risk           | High- and Critical-Risk Riders / Active Riders × 100                                                  | Weekly    | Measures portfolio risk exposure      |
| Riders with Declining Earnings | Riders whose earnings show a sustained downward trend                     | Count of riders meeting the defined earnings-decline rule                                             | Weekly    | Identifies reduced repayment capacity |
| Riders with Increasing Debt    | Riders whose outstanding balance has increased across consecutive periods | Count of riders meeting the rising-balance rule                                                       | Weekly    | Detects worsening account performance |
| Inactive Rider Count           | Riders with no recorded activity during the defined inactivity period     | Count of inactive riders                                                                              | Weekly    | Identifies operational disengagement  |
| Intervention Completion Rate   | Percentage of assigned interventions marked as completed                  | Completed Interventions / Assigned Interventions × 100                                                | Weekly    | Measures follow-up execution          |

## Calculation Notes

* Percentages should return blank or zero when the denominator is zero, depending on the reporting context.
* Financial values should use one consistent project currency.
* Negative values should only be permitted where the business rule explicitly allows them.
* Reconciliation tolerance must be documented before implementation.
* Active, idle, maintenance, repossessed and ownership-completed statuses must be mutually exclusive at a given reporting date.
* Risk thresholds and weights will be finalized before the SQL risk logic is developed.
* Every dashboard KPI must be traceable to a documented database field or reporting view.

## KPI Review and Validation

Before dashboard development, each KPI should be checked for:

1. Clear business meaning
2. Reliable source fields
3. Consistent calculation logic
4. Appropriate reporting frequency
5. Correct handling of missing or zero values
6. Traceability from source data to final result
7. Relevance to a defined business question
